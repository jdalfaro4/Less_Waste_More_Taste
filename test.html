Grandma's Chocolate Texas Sheet Cake

This Texas sheet cake is hands down my all-time favorite cake! I fell in love with making it after my grandmother shared the recipe with me. This dessert is super quick, easy, and fun to make. I definitely recommend this for first-time bakers and anyone else who would love to take a bite out of a delicious chocolate treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup salted butter
  • 1 cup water
  • 3 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

    • Directions

    Step 1 - Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 10x15-inch cake pan. Step 2 - Prepare the cake: Combine butter, water, and cocoa powder in a saucepan over medium heat. Cook until butter has melted and mixture comes to a boil. Remove from the heat and cool slightly.
